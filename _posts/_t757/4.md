>> mi toki = I speak
>> mi toki mute = mi toki<mute = I speak a lot
>> mi toki jan = mi toki<jan = I speak humanly
>> mi toki jan X = mi toki<jan<X = ???? no useful meaning
>> mi toki pi jan X = mi toki<(jan X) = \*I speak like person X\*
